Thai Massaman Curry

Rich & Flavorful with medium spiciness

Massaman curry is a traditional Thai dish with a unique and complex flavor profile. The dish is made with a blend of massaman curry paste, which includes ingredients such as cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, cumin, and nutmeg. The paste is then combined with meat (chicken, beef, or tofu), potatoes, peanuts, and coconut milk, to create a rich and creamy sauce.
